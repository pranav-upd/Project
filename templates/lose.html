Lose Weight






To find your maintenance calories, eat near your BMR (if you`ve not already calculated calculate It above) for two weeks. (the first week you may lose a lot of water weight). If youre losing weight rapidly during this time add 200 more calories and try for a week. Repeat this process until you figure out your maintenance calories (where you`re not losing much weight). If you`re gaining weight cut another 200 calories and try for a week. Repeat the process until you figure out your maintenance.


Once you figure out your maintenance calories cut 500 calories from your maintenance calories. You should start losing weight. Don`t worry if it`s not rapid. You`re slowly losing weight. If you`re not losing weight you can decrease your calorie intake by another 200 but please do not follow an extremely low calorie diet. I`d recommend you add in a workout like HIIT to burn calories and to cross the weight loss plateau.
