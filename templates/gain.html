Gain Mass






To find your maintenance calories, eat near your BMR (if you`ve not already calculated calculate It above) for two weeks. (the first week you may lose a lot of water weight). If youre losing weight rapidly during this time add 200 more calories and try for a week. Repeat this process until you figure out your maintenance calories (where you`re not losing much weight). If you`re gaining weight cut another 200 calories and try for a week. Repeat the process until you figure out your maintenance.


In order to build muscle mass you need to be in a calorie surplus. You need to add another 500 calories to your maintenance calories. Also start strength training by following any one of the programs below:-

1) AllPro

2) Jim Wendler`s 5/3/1 for beginners


If you`re not gaining mass, add another 200 calories and see for a week. You can keep repeating this until you see a visible change in your body weight. Make sure you get enough protein. The American college of sports medicine recommends you get at least 0.5 to 0.8g of protein per pound of bodyweight.If you follow this you should see that you`ve started to add muscle mass. You will inevitably gain fat too but you can burn it off once you reach your goal physique.
